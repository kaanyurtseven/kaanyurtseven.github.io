# Kaan Yurtseven

**Developing optimization tools for risk-aware decision making in future electricity grids**

I develop optimization tools that transform uncertainty in electricity grids into something we can plan for. As renewable energy grows, power systems must operate with increasingly uncertain generation and demand forecasts. My work focuses on risk-aware decision making and optimization under uncertainty, helping system operators, planners, and market participants make better decisions while enabling the reliable integration of renewable energy.

**PhD Researcher**  
KU Leuven and EnergyVille

## Research Themes

### Risk-aware decision making for power system operation and planning
Methods for supporting better decisions in complex electricity systems under risk.

### Optimization under uncertainty
Optimization frameworks that explicitly represent uncertainty and its impact on decisions.

### Renewable integration in future electricity grids
Operational and planning approaches that support the energy transition.

## Selected Highlights

- Gold Award, EERA Smart Grids PhD Candidate Award
- Research on stochastic optimization, congestion management, and hybrid AC/DC grids
- Publications and conference presentations in power systems

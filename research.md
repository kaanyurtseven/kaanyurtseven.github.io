# Research

My work sits at the intersection of optimization, uncertainty, and power system operation.

## Risk-aware decision making for power system operation and planning
I develop methods that incorporate uncertainty and risk directly into operational and planning decisions in electricity grids.

## Optimization under uncertainty
My research focuses on optimization frameworks that go beyond deterministic assumptions and explicitly account for uncertainty.

## Renewable integration in future electricity grids
I study how future grids can accommodate large shares of renewable energy while supporting robust and informed decision making.

# Research

Electricity systems are changing rapidly as renewable energy grows.  
This introduces increasing uncertainty into generation, demand, and market conditions.

My research develops **optimization tools that transform uncertainty into better decisions for electricity systems.**

---

## Research Themes

### Risk-aware decision making

Modern electricity system decisions involve risk.

My work develops optimization frameworks that allow decision makers to evaluate trade-offs between cost, reliability, and uncertainty.

Applications include:

- congestion management  
- operational planning  
- balancing and redispatch decisions

---

### Optimization under uncertainty

Traditional optimization assumes deterministic inputs.  
Future electricity systems operate under significant uncertainty.

My research develops optimization frameworks that explicitly represent uncertainty and quantify its impact on system outcomes.

This includes work on:

- stochastic optimization  
- uncertainty propagation  
- risk-aware decision frameworks

---

### Renewable integration in future electricity grids

Electricity systems must integrate large shares of renewable energy while remaining reliable and efficient.

My work studies how operational decisions, network structures, and uncertainty interact in renewable-dominated electricity systems.

---

## Methods

My research combines:

- stochastic optimization  
- risk-aware optimization models  
- power system modeling  
- uncertainty representation techniques  
- decision-support tools for complex electricity systems
